Caesar Shift Cipher, relies on shifting the letters of a message by an agreed number (three was a common choice), the recipient of this message would then shift the letters back by the same number and obtain the original message.The Caesar cipher is named after Julius Caesar , who used it with a shift of three to protect messages of military significance
